What is Peer Support?

Peer Support is a program for Year 7 and 8 students at Sancta Maria College run by their Year 11 and 12 seniors, becoming their big brothers and sisters to act as a guiding light throughout the beginnings of their school life. They learn important ideas like communication and friendship, so that they can implement them into school life as well as outside while doing exciting activities in the process.
