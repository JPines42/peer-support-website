How Does it Work?

The Peer Support sing and dance off is a fierce competition between the Peer Support classes. The sing off is for the Year 7s and the dance off for the Year 8s. The goal of the sing off is to create a parody of an already existing song to make it include the school’s values. While the dance off is just that! Showing off your classes' best moves.
